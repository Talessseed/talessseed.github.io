About me

I am a 2nd year PhD student in LaBRI, Bordeaux, under the supervison of Cyril Gavoille and Marthe Bonamy. I am in the CombAlgo department and in the GO and AlgoDist teams. You can contact me on name.surname@u-bordeaux.fr .

Research interests

I am interested in discrete mathematics and theoretical computer science, and more specifically:

  • graph theory and particularly structural graph theory,
  • parameterized algorithms,
  • and distributed computing (especially what surrounds LOCAL algorithms).
